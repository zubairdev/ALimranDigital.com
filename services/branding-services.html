Branding Services.

Research & Insights.

AI-Digital solely relies on the research and insight of your business planning. Proper research followed by development is all what we aim for when starting a branding process. Knowing your business is the first thing that we do to ensure better grip and planning of your project. Our research and development department works on the competitor analysis, pros, cons and investment opportunities that our client can afford.

Brand Strategy.

Our expert team of strategists work proficiently in planning and structuring the strategy that can bring best results and target oriented plans. Brand Strategy is very important when you want to stand apart from others in the market. Competition is to be taken care of when you have to work on the strategies which can make sure that your branding process never lags back from others. Our strategy includes Brand Position, Story & Message and architecture.

Brand Design.

At AI-Digital, the brand is everything. It’s how you stand out. It’s what your audience falls in love with. It’s what makes you matter. Every decision you make—from how you lead and the story you tell to the brand assets you create—enriches or undermines a brand’s success. We take a comprehensive approach to branding to create brands that emotionally charge your audience and help you win in the market. Our Brand Design mechanism comprises of Visual Identity, Logo marks, Iconography, Color & Type, Brand Guidelines, Tone of Voice and Brand Personality.

Visual Identity.

It is a process in which we plan how your business is viewable by your target audience. It’s how your brand looks like to your clients. Visualizing the identity is important when you want a proper pathway for your branding process. It is in our best interest to plan and structure your identity in a way that looks unique, to-the-point, attractive and beautiful.

Iconography.

The pages which are heavy with content will soon make readers switch off. Icons are very powerful visuals that can be used in the place of text and communicate with your audience. Iconography is a smart choice for the brands which aim to deliver their message in a concise format that make a solid impact on the audience.
